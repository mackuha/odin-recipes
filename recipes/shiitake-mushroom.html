Shiitake Mushroom Stir Fry

Description

In this shiitake mushroom stir fry, mushrooms, bell peppers, onion, and zucchini are glazed with a delicious sweet-salty soy-chili sauce. Add a protein if you like, and serve over rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons oyster sauce
  • Cheeses: Youâ€™ll need cottage cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sweet Thai chili sauce
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/8 teaspoon red chili pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Combine soy sauce, oyster sauce, rice vinegar, sweet Thai chili sauce, maple syrup, and red pepper chili flakes in a small bowl. Stir until smooth and set aside.
  2. Add mushrooms to a bowl, drizzle with 1 tablespoon soy sauce, and toss until coated. Sprinkle cornstarch over mushrooms and toss until completely coated.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon sesame oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms; cook until golden brown, about 5 minutes per side. Transfer mushrooms into a dish; set aside.
  4. Heat remaining 1 tablespoon sesame oil in the same wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Add onions, orange peppers, and zucchini, and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Stir sauce into pan and cook until thickened, about 3 minutes. Add mushrooms back into stir fry; toss to coat.
